## üéÆ How to Play

Now that you have installed the game, it‚Äôs time to play!

### Objective

The goal of the guess game is to correctly guess the number chosen by the computer within a limited number of attempts.

### Game Instructions

1. Launch the game by double-clicking on the icon or running the command in your terminal.
2. You will be prompted to enter your guess.
3. Type in a number and hit **Enter**.
4. The game will tell you if your guess is too high, too low, or correct.
5. Keep guessing until you find the right number!

### Tips for Play

- Start with a number in the middle of the range to narrow down your options quickly.
- Pay attention to the hints provided after each guess.
- Challenge your friends to see who can guess the number in the fewest tries!
